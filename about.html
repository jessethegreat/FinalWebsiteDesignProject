ABOUT.

Jonathan Taylor is an NFL Player who Plays Running Back for the INDIANAPOLIS COLTS. He is currently #1 in the NFL in rushing yards at 1348 yards. He leads all NFL Pro Bowl Votes with 168,222 votes. He is the best Running Back in the NFL.

Jonathan Taylor currently has the most touchdowns(5) in a game this season by a single player. He won NFL's Offensive Player of the Week this week. Folowing that month, he won his SECOND NFL Player of the Month Award for the 2nd month in a row.

Jonathan Taylor is a franchise owner of Toppers Pizza. Toppers is a Wisconsin-born pizza brand. Jonathan Taylor attend the University of Wisconsin. He was drawn to the pizza organization and their friendly environment during his time at the University of Wisconsin.
